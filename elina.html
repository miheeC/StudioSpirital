    O Elini

    "Elina v svet prinaša nežnost, intuicijo in globino."

    "Elina brings gentleness, intuition and depth into the world."

    Je izjemen medij z razvito jasnovednostjo, jasnočutnostjo in jasnovidnostjo.
    Ustvarja meditacije, izvaja individualna in skupinska kanaliziranja, Reiki terapije ter vodi vadbe (elivital® prakso). S svojim delom odpira varen prostor za notranji mir, prenaša jasnost in širi zavedanje ljudi.

    As a medium she creates meditations, conducts individual channeling sessions, leads movement classes and reiki therapies. Her work opens a space for inner peace, clarity and connection with oneself.
