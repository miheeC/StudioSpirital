    O Maji

    "Maja v Studio Spirital prinaša gibanje, dotik in življenjsko energijo."

    "Maja brings movement, touch and life energy into Studio Spirital."

    Ustvarja delavnice latina in intuitivnega plesa, skozi masaže telesa in obraza pa pomaga sproščati napetosti in vzpostavljati življenjsko ravnovesje.

    She creates Latin and intuitive dance workshops, and through body and facial massages helps release tension and restore life balance.
