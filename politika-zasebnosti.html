    Kateri podatki se obdelujejo

    Spletna stran nima kontaktnega obrazca in ne uporablja oglasnih orodij. Za anonimizirano statistiko obiska uporabljava Google Analytics (glej razdelek o piškotkih spodaj). Osebne podatke pridobiva izključno takrat, ko nas sami kontaktirate — po telefonu ali prek Instagrama/Facebooka — zaradi dogovora za termin ali povpraševanja o storitvi. Običajno gre za ime, telefonsko številko in vsebino vašega sporočila.

    The website does not have a contact form and does not use advertising tools. We use Google Analytics for anonymised visit statistics (see the cookies section below). Personal data is only obtained when you contact us yourself — by phone or via Instagram/Facebook — to arrange an appointment or ask about a service. This is typically your name, phone number and the content of your message.

    Piškotki in shramba brskalnika

    Stran uporablja storitev Google Analytics 4 (Google Ireland Ltd.), s katero meriva obisk strani — na primer, katere podstrani si obiskovalci ogledajo in s kakšne naprave. Google Analytics v vaš brskalnik shrani piškotke (_ga in _ga_*), ki veljajo do 2 let. Podatki so za naju vidni le v zbirni, statistični obliki; Google Analytics 4 ne beleži vašega polnega IP-naslova. Podatki se lahko obdelujejo tudi na Googlovih strežnikih v ZDA na podlagi okvira EU-ZDA za varstvo zasebnosti podatkov (EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework). Zbiranju lahko nasprotujete z blokiranjem piškotkov v brskalniku ali z Googlovim dodatkom za onemogočanje (tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout). Poleg tega stran v lokalno shrambo vašega brskalnika (localStorage) shrani vašo izbiro jezika (slovenščina/angleščina), da si jo zapomni ob naslednjem obisku; ta podatek ostane na vaši napravi in se nama ne pošilja.

    The site uses Google Analytics 4 (Google Ireland Ltd.) to measure how the site is used — for example, which pages visitors view and from what kind of device. Google Analytics stores cookies in your browser (_ga and _ga_*), which are kept for up to 2 years. We only see this data in aggregated, statistical form; Google Analytics 4 does not log your full IP address. Data may also be processed on Google servers in the USA under the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework. You can object to this collection by blocking cookies in your browser or by using Google's opt-out add-on (tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout). In addition, the site saves your language choice (Slovenian/English) in your browser's local storage, so it is remembered on your next visit; this data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

    Namen in pravna podlaga obdelave

    Podatke, ki nam jih posredujete, obdelujeva zato, da se lahko dogovorimo za termin, vam odgovorimo na povpraševanje in izvedemo naročeno storitev. Pravna podlaga za to je izvajanje oziroma sklepanje pogodbe (točka b prvega odstavka 6. člena Splošne uredbe o varstvu podatkov – GDPR), v preostalih primerih pa najin zakoniti interes, da odgovoriva na vaše sporočilo.

    We process the data you share with us so we can arrange your appointment, reply to your enquiry and carry out the booked service. The legal basis for this is the performance of, or steps taken prior to, a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), and in other cases our legitimate interest in responding to your message.

    Hramba in deljenje podatkov

    Osebne podatke hraniva le toliko časa, kolikor je potrebno za dogovor in izvedbo storitve, oziroma dlje, če to zahteva zakon (na primer računovodski predpisi). Podatkov ne prodajava in jih ne deliva s tretjimi osebami, razen kadar je to potrebno zaradi zakonske obveznosti. Če nam pišete prek Instagrama ali Facebooka, za samo sporočanje veljajo tudi pravila zasebnosti družbe Meta.

    We keep personal data only for as long as needed to arrange and carry out the service, or longer where required by law (for example bookkeeping rules). We do not sell your data and do not share it with third parties, except where required by law. If you message us via Instagram or Facebook, Meta's own privacy policy also applies to that communication channel.
