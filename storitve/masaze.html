    O masažah

    Masaža ni le razvajanje — je naložba v vaše zdravje, vitalnost in notranji mir.

    Massage is not just a treat — it is an investment in your health, vitality and inner peace.

    Dotik usposobljenih rok sprosti zakrčene mišice, aktivira limfni sistem in pomaga telesu, da se vrne v naravno ravnovesje. V Studio Spirital ponujamo masažo hrbta in vratu ter Glow masažo obraza — edinstveno kombinacijo ventus in maderoterapije, ki poskrbi za sijaj kože in globoko sprostitev.

    The touch of skilled hands releases tense muscles, activates the lymphatic system and helps the body return to its natural balance. At Studio Spirital we offer back and neck massage and the Glow facial massage — a unique combination of ventouse and maderotherapy that promotes glowing skin and deep relaxation.
